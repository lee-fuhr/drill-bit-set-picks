Best budget drill bit sets

Updated June 2026Independently researchedNo paid placement.

Quick answer

For most people, the DEWALT 21-piece set is the best budget-friendly all-arounder, offering a solid mix of common sizes and durable construction at a very affordable price.

If you need tougher bits for frequent heavy-duty work, the Milwaukee Shockwave 29-piece set justifies its higher cost; for tight spaces or quick fixes, the Bosch 14-piece compact set is the grab-and-go winner.

Drill bit sets are one of those tools you don’t realize you need until you’re halfway through a project and your only bit is dull or the wrong size. A good budget set covers the most common diameters (from 1/16” up to 1/2”) and includes a mix of brad-point, twist, and maybe a spade bit or two for wood, metal, and light masonry. When you’re shopping on a budget, focus on the steel type (high-speed steel is standard; cobalt or titanium coatings add durability but cost more), the quality of the case (flimsy plastic that breaks open is infuriating), and whether the set includes the sizes you actually reach for most often. Don’t overpay for 100 bits you’ll never use. A well-chosen 15- to 21-piece set will handle 90% of home and DIY tasks.
